Transits represent a vital pillar of predictive Vedic astrology. The application approaches transits dynamically through a variety of distinct tools and worksheets.

### Major Features
* **Dynamic Birth-to-Transit Mapping:** Overlays planetary transits seamlessly over the natal birth chart for concurrent evaluation.
* **Time-Engine Integration:** Synchronizes transits directly with active planetary dasha timelines to optimize event-timing accuracy.

### Minor Features
* **Event Series Worksheets:** Tailored for deep inspection of specific historical or future dates, emphasizing precise planetary alignments against natal placements.
* **Animated Transits Engine:** A real-time visual sandbox with play/pause/rewind controls, allowing users to watch planets sweep across the zodiac signs and trigger updates to the natal chart instantly.
* **Graphical Ephemeris:** Provides macro-level visual grids tracking planetary trajectories across a monthly or yearly spectrum.
* **Kaksha & Dasha Calendars:** Combines the Ashtakavarga Kaksha limits with active dasha levels to track micro-movements of planets.
* **Time of Transits Tool:** An automated search system used to pinpoint the exact millisecond a planet transitions into specific signs, houses, or degrees.
* **Transit Report Tool:** Generates chronological lists and exports printable datasets of upcoming planetary shifts.

* **Varshaphala (Tajika / Solar Return):** Computes complete annual progression charts, featuring Harsha Bala, Panchavargiya Bala, Muntha placements, and Patyayini/Mudda dasha systems.

### Minor Features
* **Muhurta Engine (Electional Time-Finder):** Step through time increments to determine ideal windows for marriage, business, and travel based on customized auspiciousness parameters.
* **Koota / Melapak Compatibility Matching:** Generates the traditional 36-point Guna Milan score alongside comprehensive evaluations of Kuja Dosha (Manglik affect) and Vedha exceptions.
* **Prashna (Horary) Quick-Caster:** Instantly generates a chart for the current time and location with a single click or keyboard shortcut; supports KP number-based seed generation.
