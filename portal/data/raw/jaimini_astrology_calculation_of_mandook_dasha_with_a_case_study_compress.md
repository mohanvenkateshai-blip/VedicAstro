JAIMINI ASTROLOGY - MANDOOK DASHA
Sanjay Rath, in his book, Jaimini Maharishi’s Upadesa Sutra, in Adhaya 2,
Ch.d,verse 15 states: “The Frog (Mandooka) dasa commences from the
ascendant or ‘“‘adarsa” sign whichever is odd and succeeding dasas will be

every third sign”. Further, he adds that Manduka means frog and the
movement is called Manduka Gati. He states that the period of the signs are :

7 years for moveable signs, 8 years for fixed signs and 9 years for dual or
common signs.

~ Dr. K.N. Rao states that ‘“mandook Dasha” is applicable to horoscopes in
which there are four or more planets (other than Rahu and Ketu) in
quadrants or kendras from the Ascendamt or lagna.

- These planets could occupy either one or two or three or all the four
kendras.

Commencement of the dashas as per Dr. K.N. Rao’s method:

- The dasha will start from the ascendant if it is an odd sign and one has to
proceed in direct order.

- The dash will start from the seventh house if the ascendant is an even sign
and one has to proceed in an indirect order.

- Mandook means frog. The dasha moves like the leap of a frog to the fourth
place.

The nature of the “frog jump” of Mandook dasha:

- If the dasha starts from Chara or Cardinal Signs, it first covers the four
cardinal signs. The next four signs could be Sthira(fixed) signs or
Dwiswabhava (common) signs.

- If the dash starts from Sthir or fixed signs, it first covers the four fixed signs.
The next four signs could be cardinal (chara) signs or Dwiswabhavi signs.

- If the dasha starts from Dwiswabavi (Common) signs, it first covers the four
common signs. The next four could either cardinal signs or fixed sings.

Savya order- if the dasha order is direct, the sub-periods for all the dashas
will follow the same order.

Apasavya order: If the dasha order is indirect, the sub-periods will follow the
same order.

e Directly in the case of odd rashis
* Indirectly in the case of even rashis.

e A planet in its own sign will give 12 years.
० A planet in the 12™ house will give 12 years.

* For Scorpio, Ketu is to be ignored and Mars taken, that too in the
reverse direction.

e If the lord of the house is in the seventh, then take ten years.
