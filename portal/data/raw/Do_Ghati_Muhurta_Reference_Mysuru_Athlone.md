Purpose. This document lists the 30 Do-Ghati Muhurat divisions for two cases, with their fixed muhurta names, corresponding nakshatra mapping, and local time slots. The nakshatra column is the traditional Do-Ghati muhurta-nakshatra correspondence; it is not the Moon's actual transit nakshatra at that moment.

Calculation rule used: Sunrise-to-sunset is divided into 15 day muhurtas; sunset-to-next-sunrise is divided into 15 night muhurtas. Therefore, a Do-Ghati Muhurat is not always exactly 48 minutes; it varies by date and location.
