2. Tithis, Their Nature, and Dagdha Rashi

A Tithi is a lunar day. Tithis are categorized into five groups based on their nature: Nanda (joy), Bhadra (auspicious),
Jaya (victory), Rikta (empty), and Purna (full). 'Dagdha Rashi' translates to 'burnt sign' and refers to zodiac signs
considered weak or afflicted based on the birth Tithi.

5. Do Ghati Muhurat (Time Cycles)

A Ghati is approx. 24 minutes. 'Do Ghati' means two Ghatis, making a 48-minute block. The day consists of 30 Do Ghati
